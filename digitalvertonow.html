Gerardo Tapia Vera: Now

I'm a front end web designer working daily to master my creativity! I do this kind of work for free from home, and I also work for East Salinas Building Healthy Communities in Monterey County as a CHW. I aspire to make a website for everyone on this planet, so they can represent themselves in the digital world. At Building Healthy Communities, my current function is to educate Monterey county individuals on the COVID-19 resources that they have. At this time, there's 3 active programs in the county for the homeless, agriculture workers, and healthcare workers to isolate and quarantine in a hotel room. The first program's called Project Roomkey, the second program's called Housing for the Harvest, and the third program's called Non-Congregate Sheltering for California Healthcare Workers. Individuals isolating or quarantining need food to be delivered to their residencies, and we provide that service for them as well. Every city has a food distribution day, and you should call your local food bank to find out which day and what time the food distribution is. The United Farm Workers Foundation has a Salinas office that distributes food every Wednesday from 3pm-5:30pm. I'm one of their volunteers, and volunteers are allowed to take a box of food home. East Salinas Building Healthy Communities also provides relief funds to Monterey county residents, so they can pay off their utility bills or rent bills. When I'm not working or volunteering, I'm usually reading Microsoft Learning Documents to learn more coding technologies that allow me to publish websites, reading non-fiction books about the CIA, spies, and information security, or playing action video games on my Xbox One like Tekken 7, King of Fighters XIII, Mortal Kombat 11, or Call of Duty Modern Warfare. If you need my services, then please visit my "Contact" page where you'll be able to send me an email or call my cell phone number. Thanks!
