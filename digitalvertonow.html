Gerardo Tapia Vera: Now

!emocleW
I'm a front end web designer working daily to master my creativity! I do this kind of work for free from home, and I also work for East Salinas Building Healthy Communities in Monterey County as a Resources Navigator. I aspire to make a website for everyone on this planet, so they can represent themselves in the digital world. At Building Healthy Communities, my current function is to find resources for all individuals that have tested positive for COVID and need a temporary location to isolate themselves and recover. At this time, there's 3 active programs in the county. The first program's called Project Roomkey, the second program's called Housing for the Harvest, and the third program's called Non-Congregate Sheltering for California Healthcare Workers. I've also found food resources for individuals living in Monterey County. Every city has a food distribution day, and you should call your local food bank to find out which day and what time the food distribution is. The United Farm Workers Foundation has a Salinas office that distributes food every Wednesday from 3pm-5:30pm. I'm one of their volunteers, and volunteers are allowed to take a box of food home. When I'm not working or volunteering, I'm usually reading Microsoft Learning Documents to learn more coding technologies that allow me to publish websites, reading non-fiction books about the CIA, spies, and information security, or playing action video games on my Xbox One like Tekken 7, King of Fighters XIII, Mortal Kombat 11, or Call of Duty Modern Warfare.
Until then!
