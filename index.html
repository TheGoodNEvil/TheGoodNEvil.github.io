About Me

I'm Digital Verto, and I'm from Salinas, California. I'll be 30 years old next year on October 17th, and I'm currently looking for a job. I'm a 5'6" Mexican-American with brown hair, brown skin, brown eyes, and an average body. I like going for walks, dancing, working out, playing soccer, and playing Modern Warfare and Ghost Recon on my Xbox One. I live in Salinas, California with family members in a full house, and the family's growing. I've had only a couple of jobs that I really liked, so I decided to learn coding and start my own business 6 months ago. My company's name's WBAMN, and it stands for Win By All Means Necessary. I'm the only employee, and I work from home. I created my company to make websites, apps, and softaware for all persons that are looking to get an advantage in the job market.

I was born and raised in Salinas, California by both of my parents until the age of 10 years old when my parents divorced, and I and my siblings decided to stay with our mother in Salinas, California. Two years later, we moved into a house in the same city and attended EL Sausal Middle School where I played soccer. It was a fun experience. Fortunately, I was also pretty good soccer player in the varsity team at Alisal High School, and at that time, even now, Alisal High School had one of the best soccer teams in the nation. I was an excellent student-athlete, and I earned academic scholarships to the University of San Francisco where I played intramural, club, and varsity soccer.

I was the only one attending a university, my siblings were growing their own families in one single household, and I would sometimes visit Salinas on the weekends to see them. They saw me very seldomly, so when they saw me, they'd all become excited. I'd become elated. My education, family, and soccer were of the most importance to me, so I focused more on graduating, family, and playing soccer. I attempted to earn a B.S. degree in Exercise and Sports Science, and that required me taking extra science classes, classes in which I failed to pass with a C or better. Thus, I settled for a B.A. and stopped playing soccer.

I earned my B.A. degree in Exercise and Sports Science from the University of San Francisco in December 2014, and I immediately came home to Salinas to help out my mother and siblings. In April 2015, I began a general labor job as a broccoli dumper, and I worked there full-time for 7 months. The job was a real workout, so I liked it. However, I knew that it wasn't what I wanted to do my entire life. I was doing a lot of physical work duiring that time, but I wasn't doing the kind of thinking I wanted to. Tragedies have occured in my home, and I've recovered from them. Six months ago, I decided to learn how to code through the Mimo app. I now have this website, and I've been learning a lot more code that I'll eventually add to this website. If you'd like to know more about me or my services, then please take a look at my entire website. My skills are displayed in my webpages. If you're impressed, then let me know. I'd love to collaborate with you towards creating a website for yourself and services. I'm currently making websites for free. In advance, thanks.
